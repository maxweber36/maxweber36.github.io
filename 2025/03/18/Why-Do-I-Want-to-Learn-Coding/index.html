💻 Why Do I Want to Learn Coding?
Mar 18, 2025

First, I believe coding can help me become more creative. It offers a pathway to escape the conventional 9-to-5 routine, leading to a freer and more creative lifestyle.

My immediate goal is to become an indie developer, which makes programming not just a fundamental skill to acquire, but in fact, among the most crucial competencies in the digital age.

What excites me most about mastering programming is the ability to freely build software solutions that create value for us. The prospect of turning ideas into functional tools that improve people’s lives gives me profounf fulfillment.

After all, isn’t creation the very essence of human existence?
